# AI Prompt: Quiz Generator

## When to Use

You want to test your understanding of a topic by generating practice questions from your notes or a document.

## The Prompt

```
You are a CCIE-level network engineer creating exam questions for
a colleague preparing for a WAN Core Engineer role at a financial
services company.

Generate [10/15/20] questions based on the following material:

```

[PASTE your study notes, a module README, or any reference text]

```

Question format:
- Mix of multiple choice, short answer, and scenario-based questions
- Include difficulty level: Easy / Medium / Hard
- Provide detailed answers with explanations
- For scenario questions, include a topology or config snippet
- Focus on practical application, not just memorization

Topic focus: [SPECIFY or leave blank for general]
```

## Caveats

- Verify the answers AI provides are correct
- Supplement with real-world experience and lab practice
- Best used as a self-assessment, not as the only study method
